Tips During COVID-19!

  • Wear a MASK when you leave the house

  • DO NOT remove your mask if you're not eating/drinking

  • Keep a 1M distancing between other people

  • For VACCINATED: Group sizes of up to 5
  • For UNVACCINATED: No Dining-in

  • Getting vaccinated is the BEST way to SLOW the spread of COVID-19

  • It is RECOMMENDED to do a ART test if you're feeling unwell

  • WASH your hands often and AVOID TOUCHING your eyes, nose, and mouth
